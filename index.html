About WARTEX

One of the most highly anticipated school tech event in Delhi.

850+ Students
55+ Schools
12 Events
1 MEGA COMPETITION

As now the world strides with the revolution of technology, we are experiencing a perpetual transofmation in our lives. To be steady with our motion of development, it is vital for the youth to grasp every facet of technology and innovation.

S.R.D.A.V. has undertaken an effort to contribute to this field of learning. Technocrats, the tech society of S.R.D.A.V. has managed to host the annual wartex event since 2015. Launching with 12 events and participation from 350+ students from 18+ schools, we have come a long way to 850+ participants from 55+ schools for 18 events in Wartex #7.0 in 2021.

And now, after a successful event last year, we are back again with the offline edition of Wartex in 2022.
